You might be wondering: what's the catch?

There isn't one. SaveWell is completely free for Medicare beneficiaries. Here's what you should know.

No enrollment fees, ever

No hidden costs, no membership fees, no fine print. SaveWell is free because the savings come from our provider partnerships, not from you.

Doctors you can trust

Every provider in our network meets quality standards for excellent patient care. We only partner with doctors we'd send our own family to.

Rewards happen automatically

Your premium credits are applied at the end of the year — no forms to fill out, no claims to file, no paperwork at all.

Your Medicare doesn't change

SaveWell works alongside your existing Medicare coverage. Nothing changes about your plan, your benefits, or your current doctors.
